About Tarangini 25

Theme - पौषृति

Annual Fest 24, the Annual Fest of Gaya College of Engineering is all set to take things to a greater level this year. With competitions in the field of dance, poetry and photography, music, dramatics and various events with a quaint twist, it promises to be an unforgettable event. Annual Fest 24 is an annual cultural festival of Gaya College of Engineering. The festival includes events like dancing, singing and ramp walk. Annual Fest 24 being the cultural part of the gathering observes the maximum number of audience support and participation, the events for sports starts from the very first day of Annual Fest and on the last day ends with a bang which is always accompanied by a popular cultural activities.
